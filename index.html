02 / Specialized

Beyond the weekly.

Sometimes the job is bigger than a vacuum and a mop. Here's what else we handle.

Water Damage Cleaning

Burst pipe, basement flood, leak you didn't catch in time. We extract, dry, and sanitize so it doesn't become a mold problem next month.

Steam Cleaning

High-temperature steam for floors, tile, grout, and upholstery. Sanitizes without harsh chemicals — the difference is visible and you can smell it.

Carpet Shampoo & Upholstery

Carpets, sofas, dining chairs — anything fabric. We lift stains, kill odors, and leave fibers feeling new instead of damp and disappointing.

Garage, Basement & Attic Clean Outs

The spaces that quietly become storage for everything you forgot. We empty them, sort what stays, and leave you a usable room.

Sanitation Cleaning

Hospital-grade disinfecting for high-touch surfaces, shared spaces, and post-illness recovery. Especially relevant for offices and rentals.

Commercial & Residential

Storefronts, offices, Airbnbs, and family homes. We scale the crew to the job — same care for a one-bedroom as a multi-floor office.
